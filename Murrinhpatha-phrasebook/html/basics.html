Basic vocabulary
▶ Nouns…  ▶ Adjectives…  ▶ Days and Times…

This section teaches you a few basic words to help you get started. These are words for talking about things in the world around you: nouns and adjectives. For some more useful beginner-level words and phrases, see Small-talk.

We don't attempt to tackle Murrinhpatha verbs (action words) in this section, because there is nothing easy or basic about them! However there is a separate Verbs section for those who would like to learn about this interesting - but difficult - aspect of the language.
